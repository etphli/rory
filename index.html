About Rory

Hello, I'm Rory Nathan, a Year 11 student at Emanuel School running for the position of Madrich. I believe in creating a school environment where every student feels valued, heard, and represented.

My vision is to foster a community that embraces our school's pluralistic values while ensuring student voices are at the heart of decision-making.

My Policies

Embracing Pluralism

Supporting and celebrating our school's pluralistic values and diverse community.

Student-Chosen Charities

Empowering students to choose which charities our school supports and donates to.

Transparency

Increasing transparency in school decision-making processes that affect students.

Student Feedback

Creating effective channels for student feedback to support school improvement.
